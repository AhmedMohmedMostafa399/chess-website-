The Chess

1- What is Chess?

Chess is a game of a real war between two countries. Each country has the same army, and the real difference is in the leader's mindset.
In chess, you think about what moves to make to win the war as an army leader. In addition, you must anticipate what the player in front of you will play.
This game is really beneficial for teenagers because it improves thinking skills.

2- The Importance of Playing Chess

Chess is one of the only games where you can see a child play against an older man and beat him.
This game is beneficial because it increases your IQ and improves your problem-solving skills.
Chess makes you think logically and calculate everything happening around you.
